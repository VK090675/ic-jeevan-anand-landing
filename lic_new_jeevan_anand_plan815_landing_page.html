How It Works

Your Journey to Financial Security

1

Premium Payment Period

You pay regular premiums for your chosen term (15 to 35 years). Choose from yearly, half-yearly, quarterly, or monthly modes. Your policy begins participating in LIC's profits through annual Simple Reversionary Bonuses.

💡 Tip: Longer terms typically accumulate higher bonuses due to compounding.

2

Maturity Payout

At the end of your policy term, you receive the Basic Sum Assured + all vested Simple Reversionary Bonuses + Final Additional Bonus (if declared by LIC) as a lump sum. Use it for retirement, children's education, or any life goal.

✅ This is YOUR money to enjoy — while protection continues for your family.

3

Post-Maturity Life Cover (Whole Life)

Here's the magic — even after receiving the full maturity amount, your life cover equal to the Basic Sum Assured continues at zero additional premium for the rest of your life. This is the whole-life component that sets Jeevan Anand apart.

🛡️ Coverage continues until death — no extra cost, no expiry date.

4

Legacy for Your Family

Whenever death occurs after maturity, your nominee receives the Basic Sum Assured as a death benefit. You've already enjoyed your maturity wealth — now your family receives a final financial cushion. That's the Jeevan Anand promise.

👨‍👩‍👧 Double benefit: You enjoy maturity + Family gets protection.
